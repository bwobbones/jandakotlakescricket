Club Information

Our Club And Our Goals.

Here at Jandakot Lakes Junior Cricket Club we want to teach boys and girls how to play Australia's National sport in a safe and fun manner.

Our club has made a strong point of ensuring that our players have coaches with a minimum level one coaching to ensure everything that your child learns is being taught the correct way. From In2Cricket to T20Blast right into Junior Cricket Jandakot Lakes believes that by teaching our players the correct way to Bat, Bowl or Field will help them to not only learn to play the game correctly but help with their confidence and enjoyment of the game.
